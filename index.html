Instructions

1) First keep your laptop straight.
2) Move approximately 3-4 feet from the laptop.
3) Move your right wrist in an ascending and descending manner, a red dot should appear on the right wrist.
4) Now press the play button, and move again move yourself 3-4 feet away from the laptop.
5) The red paddel is your right paddel, that will move as per the movement of the right wrist.
